## Video direction

- **Palette system** (from `frame.md`, capsule preset remixed to brand): cream `#FFFFFF` is the ground on every frame; ink `#777777` carries body copy, `#1E1E1E` is the universal 2px pill outline. Coral (brand navy `#263C87`) is the sole content accent — headlines/accent-lines/CTA lockup — never a headline fill. Candy hues (lime `#DEAA49`, lavender `#ADBAE8`, sky `#9EAEE4`, violet `#7A90DA`, yellow `#D99C2B`, peach `#A4B3E6`, mint `#EED3A0`) are reserved for pill fills and the one title-pill per frame — never headlines, never more than 2 per frame.
- **Motion grammar + reveal model** — long-tail `power3` eases throughout; every reveal is cued to its spoken phrase, never dumped at t=0 (Frame 1's two lines split across the VO's two clauses; Frame 3's three callout cards each arrive on their own cue). UGC/testimonial override (per BRIEF.md): lighter, more handheld motion than the cinematic default — simple fades/slides/spring-pops only, no 3D letter-tumble, no camera push-through, no WebGL. Holds are settle-only: a still read, at most a faint idle turntable drift on the product jar — never a forced breathing/pan.
- **Rhythm / held-frame allocation** — Frames 1–2 are quick punchy beats (≈2.8s / 5.2s) with a brief settle at the end of each. Frame 3 is the video's one dense beat — three callout cards accumulate beside a slowly turning product jar; this is the deliberate density exception, everything else stays sparse. Frame 4 is the held breather close: the lockup settles and holds dead-static for the back half of its 5.3s.
- **Negative list** — no device/browser mockups (physical product, not software); no flat static slideshow (front-load-then-freeze); no independent-floating "screensaver" elements; no blurred/re-colored shadow; no tenth accent color; no bold/italic-only emphasis (wrap the phrase in a candy `quote-highlight` pill instead, per `frame.md`); no invented stats or claims beyond what capture confirmed.
