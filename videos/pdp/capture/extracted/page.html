About PropDesigner Pro

Effortless Property Marketing, Designed for You.

Creating high-quality marketing materials for real estate can be time-consuming and expensive. PropDesigner Pro simplifies this process by using AI-powered technology and smart automation to generate professional brochures, exposés, and fact sheets in just a few clicks. Whether you’re an estate agent, property developer, or sales organization, our tool ensures efficient, creative, and customized document creation—saving you time while maintaining a strong brand identity.

Why Choose

Smart, Fast & Professional Property Marketing

Created Efficiently

Thanks to artificial intelligence paired with PropDesigner Pro’s special algorithm, you can create professional project brochures, exposés and fact sheets for your properties quickly and cost-effectively with just a few clicks.

Creatively Designed

You define target groups, select the right colour scheme and our tool generates professionally laid-out marketing documents in which you can adapt the content as you wish.

Customize Individually

PropDesigner Pro gives you creatively designed layout templates that you can customize individually. If you have a template for your exposés, brochures or fact sheets that you would like to use with our tool: No problem! For a one-off fee, we will create this template in PropDesigner Pro.
